Final Project Proposal: Sci-fi/Fantasy Catan

My final project will be a sci-fi themed Settlers of Catan board game that I am naming Settlers of (Something TBD). I am inspired by this magnetic and multicolored Catan-style game I found on thingiverse (shown below). This game is really cool, but I want to make my own that is 1) cast in resin and 2) themed with things I like.

Traditional Settlers of Catan has five types of resource tiles: Stone, Brick, Wood, Wheat, and Sheep. Additionally, there are ocean tiles and a central desert tile. I am going to replace these with various references to science fiction literature. I have not decided on all of them, but I want the desert tile to be a "Dune" reference, with the normal sandstorm on top of it replaced with one of the giant sand worms from Dune.

I realize that this might be a bit ambitious for two weeks. I am breaking this process down into steps:

  1. CAD pieces in Rhino
  2. Convert Pieces to one-part molds
  3. 3D print each mold
  4. Cast silicone in the molds
  5. Develop the resin color scheme by trying some different color mixtures
  6. Cast resin
  7. Design resource and development cards
  8. Plot resource and development cards on cardstock
